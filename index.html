Welcome to America!

July 4, 1776 || Red, White & Blue

Category #1: Language

The United States does not have an official language. English is the most widely used language in the U.S., and some states designate it as their official language.

Category #2: Food

Highlights of American cuisine include milkshakes, barbecue, and a wide range of fried foods. Many quintessential American dishes are unique takes on food originally from other culinary traditions, including pizza, hot dogs, and Tex-Mex.

Category #3: Fashion

Apart from professional business attire, American fashion is eclectic and predominantly informal. While Americans' diverse cultural roots are reflected in their clothing, particularly those of recent immigrants, cowboy hats, boots, jeans, and leather motorcycle jackets are emblematic of specifically American styles.
